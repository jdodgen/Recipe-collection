Mac & Cheese with Orgega chilies. Jimmy's Special
Built off of Alton Browns mac & cheese 

hardware:
    Casserole dish, typically 9"(~23cm) x 13"(~33cm) 
    Large enough pot, 4 qt minumum

Ingredients
    1 pound elbow macaroni
    3 oz butter
    6 tablespoons flour
    2 tablespoon powdered mustard
    4 cups milk (1 qt)
    3/4 cup yellow onion, finely diced
    1 bay leaf
    1+ teaspoon paprika
    1 large egg
    12+ ounces sharp cheddar (or other cheese), shredded
    1 can of mild Orgega (or other) whole green chilies 27oz         
        (optional tins of chopped)   
    1 teaspoon salt
    Fresh pepper
    Additional Topping:
        8 oz cheddar cheese
        4 oz Jack cheese

Directions:
    Preheat oven to 350F/180C
    In a large pot of boiling, salted water cook the pasta to "al dente".    
    Drain and hold
    
    in the pot, melt the butter. 
    Whisk in the flour and mustard and Whisk into a light roux. 
    Stir in the milk, onion, bay leaf, and paprika. 
    Simmer for ten minutes and remove the bay leaf.
    Season with salt and pepper
    
    Temper in the egg. 
    Stir in the cheese and let it melt
    Put the macaroni into the mix, stir
    Pour about 1/2 into the casserole dish.
    Layer the chilies and pat them in. 
    Pour the rest on top of the chilies being carefull not to disturbed the chilies . 
    Top with topping cheese.
    
    Bake for ~40 minutes I like to see the cheese toasty on top.
    Remove from oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
