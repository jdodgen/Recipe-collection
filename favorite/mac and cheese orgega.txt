Mac & Cheese with Orgega chilies. Jimmy's Special

Ingredients

    1 pound elbow macaroni
    6 tablespoons butter
    6 tablespoons flour
    2 tablespoon powdered mustard
    4 cups milk (1 qt)
    3/4 cup yellow onion, finely diced
    1 bay leaf
    1+ teaspoon paprika
    1 large egg
    12+ ounces sharp cheddar (or other cheese), shredded
    1 can of mild Orgega whole green chilies 27oz (sliced about 3/4 inch) (optional 3+ 4oz tins of chopped)
    1 teaspoon salt
    Fresh pepper

Topping:
    8 oz cheddar cheese
    4 oz Jack cheese

hardware:
    3 quart casserole dish

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350F/180C
In a large pot of boiling, salted water cook the pasta to "al dente". Drain and hold

in a pot, melt the butter. 
Whisk in the flour and mustard and Whisk into a light roux. 
Stir in the milk, onion, bay leaf, and paprika. 
Simmer for ten minutes and remove the bay leaf.
add chopped chilies
Season with salt and pepper

Temper in the egg. Stir in the cheese.
Put the macaroni into the mix, stir
Pour about 1/2 into a 3-quart casserole dish.
Layer the siced/chopped chilies and pat them in. 
Pour the rest on top of the chilies. 

Top with topping cheese.

Bake for ~40 minutes I like to see the cheese toasty on top.
Remove from oven and rest for a few minutes.
