
Hamburger Dip

2 pounds lean ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic -- minced {or 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder}
Salt to taste
15 oz. can tomato sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese -- cut in cubes
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese (or more)
1 teaspoon chili powder


In skillet, brown ground beef with onion; drain. 
Pour browned meat and onion into slow cooker. Add garlic, 
salt, tomato sauce, oregano, cream cheese, 
Parmesan cheese, and chili powder. 
Set slow cooker on Low until cream cheese has melted 
and is thoroughly blended, 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Stir 
serve with tortilla chips (I prefer Fritos).
