Mom's Potato Salad

6-7 White Potatoes
1 cup or more celery
Miracle Whip
Mustard to taste
Sweet pickle juice
Salt & Pepper
Celery Seed
4-6 boiled eggs chopped and sliced
 
 Mix all together. 
 Put Miracle Whip in until you get the moisture all over all ingredients. 
 Egg slices on top, sprinkle Paprika on top. 
 Chill overnight.
