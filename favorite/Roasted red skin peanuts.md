# Roasted Red Skin Peanuts

## Hardware
 - oven
 - baking/sheet pan.
 - partchment (optional)

## Consumables
 - red skin peanuts out of the shell.
 - spray oil
 - fine salt

 ## Process
  - Preheat oven to 350f/180c.
  - spray down pan with or without the partchment.
  - put in a single layer of peanuts
  - very lightly oil spray the peanuts.
  - Bake 10 minutes, remove stir peanuts around
  - Bake 10 minutes, remove stir peanuts around
  - Bake 10 minutes (or less), remove let them cool
  - salt and eat.
