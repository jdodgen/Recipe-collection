Hash brown casserole 

2 lbs hash brown potatoes
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cube of butter, melted
1 pint of sour cream
1 1/2 cups shredded chedder cheese
1/3 cup chopped onion
1 tsp salt
pepper
1 cup crushed potato chips mixed with 2 teaspoons melted butter

combine everything but hash brown potatoes and Potato chips, mix well
add in the hash brown potatoes

place in a 9x13 inch greased dish
top with the potato chip mixture

Refrigerate overnight

bake at 350F for 45-60 minutes, or until bubbly and crisp on top
