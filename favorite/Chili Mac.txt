Jim's Chili mac

2 15 oz cans of chili with beans
16 oz macaroni
8 oz or more of cooked spiced hamburger aka "taco meat" (cooked ahead of time, or leftover)
12 oz or more Mexican blend or other cheese.
9 x 13 inch baking dish 

cook macaroni, Al dente.
Drain and save in colander.
in the same pot heat up the cans of chili mixed with the "taco meat"
now mix in the saved macaroni
pour in to the baking dish
cover with cheese 
Bake at 350F until 160F in the center, around 40 minutes.
