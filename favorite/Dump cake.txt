Dump Cake

1 can crushed pineapple
1 can cherry pie filling
1 box white cake mix
1/2+ cup walnuts, roughly chopped
1 1/2 sticks butter sliced thinly into pats

PREPARATION
Preheat oven to 350ÂºF and lightly grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking dish, with butter or non-stick spray
Spread crushed pineapple in an even layer across baking dish
Spread cherry pie filling carefully on top of crushed pineapple 
Sprinkle all of the cake mix on top of cherry layer
Top cake mix with an even layer of butter pats 
Top with walnuts
Bake for 1 hour at 350
Remove from oven and let cool 15 minutes before serving
