Virginia City Jeep Possie "Bean’s"

2 clove garlic 
2 lbs hamburger 
2 lbs sausage 
2 jalapeño 
2 onions
2 lb pinto beans 
1 lb bacon 
Chili powder 
Soak beans overnight and cook as directed 
Add ingredients and simmer all day.

Meat to bean ratio seemed high, add more beans next time or reduce meat.
